IT & TEL INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY & TELECOMMUNICATIONS

We were pioneers in implementing what is known today in the Argentine telecommunications industry as “Neutral Networks” or “Telco Infrastructure Sharing”.

Developments that we have been implementing and perfecting for over 10 years in various jurisdictions and network topologies.

Operating on the same passive infrastructure, allowing the coexistence of MAN, WAN, and LAN networks with transmission from both fixed and mobile TELCOs.


DIFFERENTIATION STRATEGY

We adapt with Synergy to the operation of our Location Providers: we coordinate and support all network access (site and fiber optic), ensuring the proper operation of the space owner as well as access for telecommunications operators.

This strategy is reflected in our agreements, and together with the engineering of our networks, it allows us to provide the highest Service Level Agreement (SLA) in the market.

Other aspects that characterize us are the visual and environmental care of our implementations, sustainably certifying each project.
